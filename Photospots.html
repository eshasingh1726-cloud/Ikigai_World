Valley of Flowers, Uttarakhand

The Valley of Flowers National Park, nestled in the Western Himalayas, transforms into a breathtaking canvas of endemic alpine flowers during the monsoon season. This UNESCO World Heritage site stretches over 87 square kilometers and is home to rare and endangered animals like the Asiatic black bear, snow leopard, and blue sheep. Discovered in 1931 by British mountaineers, the valley is accessible through a scenic trek from Govindghat.

Essential Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

India's climate varies dramatically by region. Generally, October to March offers pleasant weather across most of the country. Mountain regions are best visited during summer (May-September), while beaches are ideal during winter.

Travel Documents

Most visitors need a valid passport and visa. E-Visas are available for many nationalities. For certain protected areas like Ladakh and Northeast India, additional permits may be required.

Getting Around

India has extensive rail and bus networks. For remote areas, consider hiring a driver. Domestic flights connect major cities. Ride-hailing apps work well in urban areas. Always plan for extra travel time.
