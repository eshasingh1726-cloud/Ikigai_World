Taj Mahal, Agra

An iconic symbol of love, this white marble mausoleum was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It's a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Red Fort, Delhi

Built by Emperor Shah Jahan when he moved his capital from Agra to Delhi, this magnificent red sandstone fort served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors for nearly 200 years.
