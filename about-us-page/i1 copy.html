Your Rajasthan Itinerary

Here's your personalized 5-day journey through the colorful land of Rajasthan. Discover majestic forts, vibrant markets, and royal heritage.

Day 1: Jaipur - The Pink City

9:00 AM Amber Fort

Start your day with a visit to the magnificent Amber Fort. Don't miss the elephant ride to the entrance and the stunning Mirror Palace.

1:00 PM Lunch at Rajasthani Restaurant

Enjoy authentic Rajasthani cuisine at a local restaurant. Try Dal Baati Churma and Gatte ki Sabzi.

3:00 PM City Palace & Jantar Mantar

Explore the City Palace complex and the astronomical observatory of Jantar Mantar, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

6:00 PM Shopping at Johari Bazaar

Experience the vibrant markets of Jaipur. Shop for textiles, jewelry, and handicrafts.

8:00 PM Dinner & Cultural Show

End your day with a traditional Rajasthani dinner accompanied by folk music and dance performances.

Day 2: Jodhpur - The Blue City

8:00 AM Travel to Jodhpur

Morning departure for Jodhpur. Enjoy the scenic drive through the Rajasthani countryside.

12:00 PM Mehrangarh Fort

Visit the imposing Mehrangarh Fort that towers over the blue city. Explore its museums and enjoy panoramic views.

4:00 PM Blue City Walk

Take a guided walking tour through the blue-painted houses of the old city, learning about local traditions and daily life.

7:00 PM Dinner at Rooftop Restaurant

Enjoy dinner at a rooftop restaurant with views of the illuminated fort and city skyline.

Day 3: Udaipur - City of Lakes

10:00 AM City Palace

Explore the grand City Palace complex with its museums, courtyards, and beautiful lake views.

1:00 PM Boat Ride on Lake Pichola

Enjoy a serene boat ride on Lake Pichola, passing by Jag Mandir and the Lake Palace.

3:00 PM Saheliyon Ki Bari

Stroll through the historic garden built for royal ladies, featuring fountains, marble pavilions, and lotus pools.

7:00 PM Cultural Show at Bagore Ki Haveli

Witness traditional Rajasthani dance and puppet shows at this heritage haveli by the lake.

Day 4: Pushkar - Holy Town

8:00 AM Travel to Pushkar

Drive to the sacred town of Pushkar, known for its Brahma Temple and tranquil atmosphere.

12:00 PM Visit Brahma Temple

Visit one of the few temples dedicated to Lord Brahma in the world.

2:00 PM Walk Around Pushkar Lake

Take a peaceful walk around the ghats of Pushkar Lake, observing religious rituals and local life.

5:00 PM Camel Safari

Experience a desert camel ride during sunset, a unique highlight of Rajasthan.

Day 5: Ajmer & Return

9:00 AM Ajmer Sharif Dargah

Visit the revered Sufi shrine of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti in Ajmer.

11:00 AM Local Markets

Explore Ajmer's local markets for traditional handicrafts and souvenirs.

2:00 PM Departure

Begin your return journey with cherished memories of Rajasthan.
