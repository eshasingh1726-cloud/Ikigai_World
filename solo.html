Solo Travel Safety in India

Essential Safety Tips

  • Research your destinations thoroughly before traveling
  • Share your itinerary with family or friends
  • Keep digital and physical copies of important documents
  • Dress modestly, especially in religious sites and rural areas
  • Use reliable transportation services, preferably pre-booked
  • Stay in well-reviewed accommodations with good security
  • Purchase comprehensive travel insurance
  • Register with your country's embassy in India

Useful Apps for Solo Travelers

  • Ola/Uber - Safe and reliable transportation
  • Maps.me - Offline maps for navigation
  • Hostelworld/Zostel - For solo traveler-friendly accommodation
  • Google Translate - For language barriers
  • Emergency contact apps - Local emergency numbers
  • TraveLibro - Connect with other solo travelers
  • Touristlink - Meet travel companions along the way

For Women Solo Travelers

  • Consider booking women-only accommodations where available
  • Use women's compartments in trains when traveling by rail
  • Be assertive and confident in your interactions
  • Trust your intuition and remove yourself from uncomfortable situations
  • Consider joining group tours for certain destinations

Recommended Destinations for Women Solo Travelers:
Rishikesh, Pondicherry, Mysore, Goa (North), Udaipur, Hampi, McLeod Ganj, Auroville, and Kerala backwaters offer relatively safer environments with established tourist infrastructure.
